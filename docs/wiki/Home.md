## What ViTransfer is
ViTransfer is a self-hosted video and photo review platform for filmmakers, photographers, and creative teams. It lets you upload originals, generate watermarked previews, share secure client links, collect timestamped feedback (video) or pin-based feedback (photos), and manage approval workflows.

## Core concepts
- Projects contain videos or photos, version history, and client recipients.
- Projects are either **Video** projects or **Photo** projects, each with a dedicated layout.
- Clients use share links to view, comment, and approve.
- Admins manage settings, security, analytics, and notifications.
- The worker handles background processing (transcoding + notifications).
